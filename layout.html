What's in it for me?

Go from learning to implementing to contributing and eventually earning

We know it’s not easy to turn your idea into writeups and sucessful projects. Well, sudoRun Labs is here to help. We have an active group of collaboraters, moderators, contributors and mentors who are always available for you to discuss your doubts.

Read Tutorials

Tools, resources & inspiration

Joining sudoRun Labs gives you access to a portfolio of development platforms for Wearables and internet of Things (IoT). We provide SDKs and HDKs as well as developer guides, tutorials and example code. There is also a free cloud sandbox service to manage your prototype-device data.

Technical support

sudoRun Labs has active member forums that provide help and insights from both the community and sudoRun Labs’ own experts. Registered companies also get access to dedicated e-mail support.

Developer advice & matchmaking

sudoRun is an opensource community of developers, students, contributors and mentors. All codes and tutorials are available on our github repository. Anyone can go and edit it to make it better and then send a pull request. The moderators will then review the request and merge it accordingly.
